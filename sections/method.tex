\section{Method}
\subsection{Overview}

To address the sample efficiency problem in the mixed-autonomy routing problem, we propose UrbanDreamer, a model-based reinforcement learning method. Instead of improving a CAV routing policy only through repeated real-world interactions, UrbanDreamer learns a latent world model of
traffic dynamics transitions and then trains the CAV actor through imagined
rollouts.
% 中文注释：UrbanDreamer 是一种用于 mixed-autonomy route control 的 model-based reinforcement learning 方法。它不是只通过反复 microscopic simulation 来改进 CAV routing policy，而是先学习一个紧凑的 segment-node traffic evolution world model，然后通过 imagined rollouts 训练 CAV actor。Simulator interaction 仍然用于 data collection 和 policy selection，但大多数 policy updates 在学习到的 latent traffic space 中完成。

For modeling this problem,  we extract road segment traffic field \(X_t\) and vehicle observation
\(\{o_{i,t}\}_{i\in\mathcal{I}_t}\) from the global state \(s_t\). The method is organized around two representations. Here, the road segment traffic field \(X_t\) is used for transition learning, while the vehicle observation
\(\{o_{i,t}\}\) is used by the CAV actor.
% 中文注释：该方法围绕两种表示组织。第一种是 segment-node traffic field，其中 \(G=(N,E)\) 的每个 node 表示一条 road segment，并携带 speed 和 occupancy 等动态交通量。第二种是 vehicle-level route decision，其中每个 active CAV 从当前 segment 到 destination 的 candidate routes 中选择一条。UrbanDreamer 通过 planned CAV pressure 连接这两种表示：实际采样的 one-hot route decisions 被投影到 road-segment graph 上，而这个 graph field 作为 world model 的条件。
With these observations, UrbanDreamer maps the MDP transition into a latent
prediction problem:
\begin{equation}
  \begin{array}{rcl}
  Z_t &=& f_\phi(X_t,G),\\
  \Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}} &=& \Pi_G(\mathbf a_t,\mathcal{I}_t),\\
  \hat Z_{t+1}
  &=&
  F_\phi(
  Z_t; \Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}},D_t,H_t^{\mathrm{HDV}},C_t
  ),
  \end{array}
\end{equation}
where \(\mathbf a_t\) is the joint route action of the active CAVs, \(D_t\) is
the departure demand, \(H_t^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) is the HDV traffic influence, and
\(C_t\) is an episode context containing the normalized decision time and the
active CAV and HDV fractions.
% 中文注释：基于这些 observations，UrbanDreamer 将 MDP transition 映射为一个 latent prediction problem：\(Z_t=f_\phi(X_t,G)\)，\(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}=\Pi_G(\mathbf a_t,\mathcal{I}_t)\)，并由 \(F_\phi\) 在 \(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}\)、\(D_t\)、\(H_t^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) 和 episode context \(C_t\) 的条件下预测 \(\hat Z_{t+1}\)。其中 \(\mathbf a_t\) 是 active CAVs 实际执行的 joint route action，\(C_t\) 包含归一化决策时间和 active CAV/HDV 比例。learned transition 不是新的 problem definition，而是 policy learning 中使用的 MDP transition surrogate。

    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.75\linewidth]{figure/overview.png}
    \caption{Overview}
    \label{fig:overview}
\end{figure}

The rest of the method introduces the main modules of UrbanDreamer. The
Flow Matching Dynamics Model learns the transition \(F_\phi\) over the latent space. The HDV Response Module estimates the uncontrollable human-driven
traffic influence \(H_t^{\mathrm{HDV}}\).  The Actor-Critic Learner
optimizes the CAV route actor \(\pi_\theta\) using imagined rollouts and long-horizon value signals. Finally, we will explain the Training Pipeline of this system.
% 中文注释：method 的其余部分直接来自这一映射。flow-matching-based segment world model 在 segment-node latents 上学习 \(p_\phi\)。HDV response module 估计不可控的 human-driven traffic context \(H_t^{\mathrm{HDV}}\)。two-level actor-critic learner 使用 imagined rollouts 和 centralized value signals 优化 CAV route actor \(\pi_\theta\)。最后，simulator evaluation 选择 actor checkpoints，因此 policy improvement 仍然绑定在真实 MDP 上，而不是只绑定在 imagined returns 上。

\subsection{Flow Matching Dynamics Model}

UrbanDreamer builds the dynamics transition with four components: A GNN encoder compresses the segment traffic
field \(X_t\) into a latent representation \(Z_t\). A decoder grounds the
latent representation in traffic attributes, which later supply
rewards and value targets for imagined rollouts. An action encoder
aggregates vehicle-level route decisions into graph-level planned
pressure. And a flow-matching model predicts latent transitions.
% 中文注释：本段是 3.2 的路线图段：先声明设计逻辑——想象训练的质量受限于 transition 的质量，然后按 encoder→conditioning→flow-matching transition→readouts 的顺序给出组件地图，并与 Overview 中的 \(F_\phi\) 记号衔接。写作功能是让读者在进入公式前掌握世界模型的整体结构。

\subsubsection{GNN Traffic Encoder}

Given the traffic field \(X_t\) and the adjacency matrix \(A\) of the road
graph, a stack of GNN message-passing layers
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
H^0 &=& X_tW_x,\\
H^{\ell+1} &=&
\mathrm{LN}\!\left(
H^\ell+\sigma(AH^\ell W_m+H^\ell W_s)
\right)
\end{array}
\end{equation}
produces the latent traffic representation \(Z_t=H^L\), where \(W_x,W_m,W_s\) are learned projections, \(\sigma\) is a
nonlinear activation function, and \(\mathrm{LN}\) denotes layer normalization.
Each row \(z_{t,u}=Z_t(u)\) summarizes road segment \(u\) together with its
graph neighborhood. 
% 中文注释：本段先给出设计目的（拥堵传播天然发生在路网图上，所以编码器要在 segment-node graph 上做消息传递），再给出 GNN 编码公式。\(z_{t,u}\) 概括了 segment \(u\) 及其邻域。写作功能：先讲 why 再讲 what，避免读者把编码器当成任意的实现选择。

\subsubsection{Reconstruction Decoder}

Let \(x^{\mathrm{dyn}}_{t,u}\) denote the dynamically observed vector of segment
\(u\), containing occupancy \(c_{t,u}\) and normalized speed \(v_{t,u}\). UrbanDreamer decodes these attributes from \(Z_t\) with SiLU activation followed by separate linear channel heads:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\hat x^{\mathrm{dyn}}_{t,u}
=
[\hat c_{t,u},\hat v_{t,u}] =
h_\rho^x(\mathrm{SiLU}(W_d z_{t,u})).
\end{array}
\end{equation}

The decoder is trained jointly with the dynamics model by adding the  reconstruction loss on observed traffic fields:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{rec}}
&=&
\displaystyle
\frac{1}{M}\sum_{u\in N}
\|\hat x^{\mathrm{dyn}}_{t,u}-x^{\mathrm{dyn}}_{t,u}\|^2.
\end{array}
\end{equation}
In actor-critic learning, the decoder is also used to calculate route-level episode rewards and value targets.
% 中文注释：只有当 control 所需的 traffic quantities 可以从 latent representation 中重构出来时，该 representation 才能支持 policy learning。令 \(x^{\mathrm{dyn}}_{t,u}\) 表示 segment \(u\) 的动态观测向量，包括 occupancy、normalized speed、vehicle count 和 halting count。UrbanDreamer 通过 shared affine map 和 SiLU activation，再接独立的 linear channel heads，从 \(Z_t\) 中解码这些量。volume channel 在作为物理非负 volume 使用前会经过 softplus 映射。decoded speed 和 volume 随后提供 imagined rewards 与 route-cost features 使用的 travel-time readouts。decoder 通过 observed traffic fields 上的 reconstruction loss \(\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{dec}}\) 训练；在 online world-model training 中，同一个 decoder 也用于监督 flow-matching transition 之后预测出的 next-step latents。这个 autoencoding constraint 防止 latent traffic representation 丢弃后续 imagined rollouts 中计算 rewards、route-cost features 和 value targets 所需的 observable traffic variables。


\subsubsection{Action Encoder}

The transition model evolves graph-level traffic representations \(Z_t\), but
decisions are made at the vehicle level: each active CAV
selects one candidate route from its local observation. So we construct
an action representation to connect the vehicle-level route decisions and graph-level traffic transitions. 


We project the realized one-hot CAV route decisions onto the segment
nodes that those routes are expected to visit. The result is a graph-level
planned pressure field \(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}\), defined on the
same road segment set as \(X_t\) and \(Z_t\).
% 中文注释：transition model 推进的是 graph-level traffic representations \(Z_t\)，但可控决策发生在 vehicle level：每个 active CAV 根据自己的 local observation 选择一条 candidate route。因此，UrbanDreamer 需要一个 action representation，将 vehicle-level route decisions 和 graph-level traffic transitions 放到同一个坐标系统中。我们通过把 CAV route probabilities 投影到这些 routes 预计会经过的 segment nodes 上来构造该表示。得到的结果是在与 \(X_t\) 和 \(Z_t\) 相同的 segment-node set 上定义的 graph-level planned pressure field \(A_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}\in\mathbb{R}^M\)。这使 CAV action 能够被 world model 使用，同时不替换 individual vehicles 在执行时使用的 route-choice interface。
For active CAV \(i\), let \(\mathrm{Seg}_{\Delta}(P^k_{i,t})\subseteq
N\)
denote the segment nodes that vehicle \(i\) would enter within the
look-ahead window \(\Delta\) if it followed candidate route
\(P^k_{i,t}\). The planned pressure averages route indicators over the active
set:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}(u)
=
\displaystyle
\frac{1}{\max(|\mathcal{I}_t|,1)}
\sum_{i\in\mathcal{I}_t}\sum_{k=1}^{K}
\mathbf{1}[k=a_{i,t}]
\displaystyle
\,\xi_{i,t}^{k,\Delta}(u),\\
\xi_{i,t}^{k,\Delta}(u)
=
\left\{
\begin{array}{ll}
1, & u\in\mathrm{Seg}_{\Delta}(P^k_{i,t})\\
0, & \mathrm{otherwise}
\end{array}
\right.
\end{array}
\end{equation}
The same sampled one-hot route is executed by the vehicle, stored in replay,
and supplied to the transition condition, so \(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}\) always
matches the physical intervention.  The mean normalization states where the
controlled demand intends to go; the absolute controlled volume enters the
transition separately through the active-CAV fraction in \(C_t\).


% 中文注释：本段给出 planned CAV pressure 的定义：对每辆 active CAV，统计其实际采样的 one-hot route 在 look-ahead window \(\Delta\) 内将进入的 segment 集合，对 active CAV 数取平均（分母用 \(\max(|\mathcal I_t|,1)\) 防空集）。执行、replay 和 transition 条件使用同一个 sampled one-hot，保证 action alignment；绝对控制流量由 \(C_t\) 中的 active-CAV 比例单独进入 transition。


\subsubsection{State-to-State Flow Matching}

UrbanDreamer learns the latent transition with state-to-state flow
matching. The transition condition consists of planned CAV pressure,
departure demand, HDV influence, and episode context:
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{C}_t=
(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}},D_t,H_t^{\mathrm{HDV}},C_t),
\end{equation}
where \(C_t=[\,t/T,\ |\mathcal{I}_t|/|N^{\mathrm{CAV}}|,\
|\mathcal{N}_t^{\mathrm{HDV}}|/|N^{\mathrm{HDV}}|\,]\) carries the normalized
decision time and the active CAV and HDV fractions.
Instead of regressing \(Z_{t+1}\) directly, the model learns a velocity field
that transports the current latent representation to the next one under
\(\mathcal{C}_t\). For a real transition pair \((Z_t,Z_{t+1})\), sample
\(\tau\sim\mathcal{U}(0,1)\) and define
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
x_\tau &=& (1-\tau)Z_t+\tau Z_{t+1},\\
\nu_{t,\tau} &=& \frac{dx_\tau}{d\tau} = Z_{t+1}-Z_t. 
\end{array}
\end{equation}
The previous latent traffic representation \(Z_t\) is the flow
source, and \(\nu_{t,\tau}\) is the target velocity.  This is a deterministic
state-to-state transport in the spirit of rectified
flow \cite{lipman2023flow,liu2023rectified}: the straight interpolation path
keeps multi-step imagined rollouts stable, and we do not claim multimodal
generative modeling of \(Z_{t+1}\).
% 中文注释：UrbanDreamer 使用 state-to-state flow matching 学习一步 latent transition。transition condition 由 planned CAV pressure、departure demand、HDV response 和 episode context 组成，即 \(\mathcal{C}_t=(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}},D_t,H_t^{\mathrm{HDV}},C_t)\)。模型不是直接回归 \(Z_{t+1}\)，而是在 \(\mathcal{C}_t\) 条件下学习一个 flow-matching velocity field，将当前 latent representation 推进到下一步。这个损失在优化时不需要 simulator rollout，推理时可以通过 integration steps 在精度和计算量之间权衡，同时 transition 仍然是强条件的一步模型，而不是开放式 sequence generator。这里 \(t\) 表示 traffic decision epoch，而 \(\tau\in[0,1]\) 表示连续 flow path。对于真实 transition pair \((Z_t,Z_{t+1})\)，采样 \(\tau\sim\mathcal{U}(0,1)\)，定义 \(x_0=Z_t\)、\(x_1=Z_{t+1}\)、\(x_\tau=(1-\tau)x_0+\tau x_1\)、\(\nu^\star_{t,\tau}=x_1-x_0\)。因此，previous latent traffic representation \(Z_t\) 是 flow source \(x_0\)，而不是额外提供给 velocity predictor 的输入。

We parameterize the velocity predictor \(\nu_\phi\) with a block-causal
transformer\cite{vaswani2017attention} over road segment traffic representations. At each decision
epoch, the network receives the interpolated representation \(x_\tau\), time condition \(\tau\), and transition condition \(\mathcal{C}_t\). The transformer block mixes information through
causal attention over previous traffic representations. A linear output head
maps the resulting representations to the velocity estimate:
\begin{equation}
\hat\nu_{t,\tau}=\nu_\phi(x_\tau,\tau,\mathcal{C}_t).
\end{equation}
% 中文注释：我们使用 segment-node traffic representations 上的 block-causal Transformer 来参数化 velocity predictor \(\nu_\phi\)。在每个 decision epoch，网络接收插值后的 representation \(x_\tau\)、\(\tau\) 的 flow-time embedding，以及由 \(\mathcal{C}_t\) 得到的 graph-aligned condition representations。Transformer 在同一步内混合 segment nodes 之间的信息，并通过 previous traffic representations 上的 causal attention 传播 temporal context。线性输出头将得到的 representations 映射为 velocity estimate \(\hat\nu_{t,\tau}=\nu_\phi(x_\tau,\tau,\mathcal{C}_t)\)。
Because most road segments are empty most of the time, an
unweighted objective would be dominated by nearly unchanged segment states. The training
loss therefore applies segment weights \(w_{t,u}\) that emphasize
occupied and congested segments while bounding the maximum weight to prevent
degenerate focusing:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{l}
\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{FM}}
=
{E}_{t,\tau}
\left[
\sum_{u\in N}w_{t,u}\|\hat\nu_{t,\tau,u}-\nu_{t,\tau,u}\|^2
\right],
\end{array}
\end{equation}
% 中文注释：本段给出 velocity-field prediction 与加权 flow-matching 损失。残差 \(\delta_{t,\tau,u}\) 比较模型在 decision epoch \(t\)、flow time \(\tau\)、segment \(u\) 上预测的 velocity 和目标 velocity \(\nu^\star_{t,\tau,u}\)。权重 \(w_{t,u}\) 偏向有车和拥堵的 segment 并设上限防止退化聚焦；动机是绝大多数 segment 长期空闲，不加权会被自由流预测淹没。损失本身不需要模拟器 rollout。

At imagination time, \(Z_{t+1}\) is unavailable, the next latent traffic representation is obtained by
integrating the learned velocity field:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\hat Z_{t+1}
&=&
\displaystyle
Z_t+\int_0^1
\nu_\phi(\hat x_\tau,\tau,\mathcal{C}_t)\,d\tau.
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Multi-step imagination repeatedly applies this one-step transition, using
each predicted \(\hat Z_{t+1}\) as the next source representation.
% 中文注释：本段说明想象阶段的推理方式：从 \(x_0=Z_t\) 出发对速度场做 ODE 积分得到 \(\hat Z_{t+1}\)；多步想象反复应用该一步 transition，把每次预测作为下一步的流源。写作功能：闭合 3.2 的训练—推理回路。


\subsection{HDV Response Module}

HDVs choose routes at departure time according to their own travel intent, and
adapt across episodes to the congestion that CAV routing induces.  UrbanDreamer
models the two time scales of this interaction separately.  Within an episode,
the learned transition responds to the current CAV pressure
\(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}\) at every decision epoch.  Across episodes, human
adaptation is slower than a single route decision, so it is represented as an
episode-level response field over road segments that is predicted from the
previous episode and held fixed within each imagined episode.  Imagined
rollouts therefore optimize against step-level counterfactual traffic
responses, while day-to-day HDV demand shifts are learned from real simulator
episodes rather than simulated inside imagination.

\subsubsection{Response Target}

We represent the HDV influence as the road-segment distribution
\(H^{\mathrm{HDV}}\), where each entry summarizes how strongly
the human-driven traffic background is associated with one road segment. Let \(h^{\mathrm{HDV}}_t\) denote the observed HDV
background field over road segments at decision epoch \(t\).  For an observed
episode of length \(T\), the supervision target is obtained by aggregating
this background field over time and normalizing it across road segments:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
H^\star
&=&
\mathrm{Norm}\!\left(
\displaystyle\frac{1}{T}\sum_{t=1}^{T} h^{\mathrm{HDV}}_t
\right),
\end{array}
\end{equation}
where \(\mathrm{Norm}\) clips negative entries and rescales the vector to sum
to one. \(H^\star\) is the 
HDV response field that summarizes the realized HDV traffic pattern
under the episode-level CAV pressure pattern.
% 中文注释：令 \(h^{\mathrm{HDV}}_t\in\mathbb{R}_{\ge0}^{M}\) 表示在 decision epoch \(t\) 观测到的 road segments 上的 HDV background field。对于长度为 \(T\) 的 observed episode，supervision target 通过在时间上聚合这个 background field 并在 road segments 上归一化得到：\(H^\star=\mathrm{Norm}(\frac{1}{T}\sum_{t=1}^{T}h^{\mathrm{HDV}}_t)\)。其中 \(\mathrm{Norm}\) 会裁剪负值并把向量重新缩放到和为 1。因此，\(H^\star\) 不是 per-vehicle route label；它是一个 slow background-response field，概括了 episode-level CAV pressure pattern 下实际形成的 HDV traffic pattern。


\subsubsection{Auxiliary Response Prediction}

The response predictor estimates how the HDV background evolves from one
episode to the next after observing the previous episode's CAV pressure and
HDV state.  For episode \(e\), we first summarize the CAV pressure in the
previous episode:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\bar\Psi_{e-1}^{\mathrm{CAV}}
&=&
\mathrm{Norm}\!\left(
\displaystyle\frac{1}{T_{e-1}}
\sum_{t=0}^{T_{e-1}-1}\Psi_{e-1,t}^{\mathrm{CAV}}
\right),
\end{array}
\end{equation}
and combine it with the previous HDV background
\(H_{e-1}^{\mathrm{HDV}}\). The HDV response predictor is an MLP \(f_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}\)
followed by a softmax over road segments:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\hat H_e^{\mathrm{HDV}}
&=&
g_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}
\!\left(
\bar\Psi_{e-1}^{\mathrm{CAV}},
H_{e-1}^{\mathrm{HDV}}
\right),\\[0.35em]
g_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}(\cdot)
&=&
\mathrm{softmax}\!\left(f_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}(\cdot)\right).
\end{array}
\end{equation}
During supervised training,
the response loss is a soft-label
cross entropy over adjacent episode pairs:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{resp}}
&=&
\displaystyle
-
\frac{1}{|\mathcal{E}_{\mathrm{pair}}|}
\sum_{e\in\mathcal{E}_{\mathrm{pair}}}
\sum_{u\in N}
H_e^\star(u)
\log \hat H_e^{\mathrm{HDV}}(u).
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Here \(\mathcal{E}_{\mathrm{pair}}\) is the set of training episodes whose
previous episode is available. \(H_e^\star\) is the target HDV response field.
The response predictor and the base world model are optimized by two
independent objectives with separate optimizers:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\mathcal{L}_{\phi}
&=&
\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{FM}}
+
\lambda_{\mathrm{rec}}\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{rec}},\\[0.35em]
\mathcal{L}_{\psi}
&=&
\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{resp}}.
\end{array}
\end{equation}
\(\mathcal{L}_{\phi}\) updates the encoder, flow dynamics, and decoder, while
\(\mathcal{L}_{\psi}\) updates only the response predictor \(g_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}\).
This auxiliary predictor gives the world model a dedicated channel for
slow HDV adaptation across episodes, while preserving the modeling assumption
that HDVs are not controlled by the learned CAV policy.
% 中文注释：response predictor 估计 HDV background 在观察上一 episode 的 CAV pressure 和 HDV state 之后如何从一个 episode 演化到下一个 episode。对于 episode \(e\)，我们首先概括上一 episode 中的 CAV pressure：\(\bar\Psi_{e-1}^{\mathrm{CAV}}=\mathrm{Norm}(\frac{1}{T_{e-1}}\sum_{t=0}^{T_{e-1}-1}\Psi_{e-1,t}^{\mathrm{CAV}})\)，并将其与 previous HDV background \(H_{e-1}^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) 和 episode context \(\rho_e\) 结合。在我们的实现中，\(\rho_e\) 是 online response predictor 使用的 normalized episode progress。predictor 是一个 MLP \(f_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}\)，后接 road segments 上的 softmax：\(\hat H_e^{\mathrm{HDV}}=g_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}(\bar\Psi_{e-1}^{\mathrm{CAV}},H_{e-1}^{\mathrm{HDV}},\rho_e)\)，且 \(g_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}(\cdot)=\mathrm{softmax}(f_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}(\cdot))\)。在 supervised training 中，\(H_{e-1}^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) 是 observed previous target \(H_{e-1}^\star\)，prediction target 是 \(H_e^\star\)。在 online prediction time，\(H_{e-1}^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) 是最近可用的 previous-episode HDV state。response loss 是 adjacent episode pairs 上的 soft-label cross entropy：\(\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{resp}}=-\frac{1}{|\mathcal{E}_{\mathrm{pair}}|}\sum_{e\in\mathcal{E}_{\mathrm{pair}}}\sum_{u\in N}H_e^\star(u)\log\hat H_e^{\mathrm{HDV}}(u)\)。这里 \(\mathcal{E}_{\mathrm{pair}}\) 是 previous episode 可用的 training episodes 集合。response module 作为 training pipeline 的 auxiliary component 优化；它的 loss 与 transition matching 和 representation reconstruction 一起构成整体 learned-model objective：\(\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{WM}}=\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{FM}}+\lambda_{\mathrm{dec}}\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{dec}}+\lambda_{\mathrm{resp}}\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{resp}}\)。这个 auxiliary objective 为 world model 提供了专门建模跨 episode 的 slow HDV adaptation 的通道，同时保留 HDV 不受 learned CAV policy 控制的建模假设。
