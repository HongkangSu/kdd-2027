\subsection{Actor-Critic Learner}

\begin{figure}
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.75\linewidth]{figure/actor-critic.png}
    \caption{actor-critic}
    \label{fig:actor-critic}
\end{figure}
To optimize the route policy, UrbanDreamer combines a parameter-shared decentralized actor with a
per-vehicle critic. The actor selects a route from each CAV's local
candidate set at execution time, while the critic jointly processes
the active CAV observations during training and returns a separate value for
each vehicle.  A global critic is retained as an auxiliary estimator of the
global mean travel time.
% 中文注释：world model 推进 graph-level traffic representation \(Z_t\)，而 route actions、route progress 和 arrivals 都以 individual CAVs 为索引。若只用一个 graph-level value 概括 imagined rollout，就会丢失不同车辆在 destinations、remaining routes 和 termination times 上的差异。因此，UrbanDreamer 将 parameter-shared decentralized actor 与 centralized per-vehicle critic 结合起来。执行时，actor 从每辆 CAV 的 local candidate set 中选择 route；训练时，per-vehicle critic 联合处理 active CAV observations，并为每辆车分别输出一个 value。我们同时保留一个 global critic，作为 network-level rollout value 的辅助估计器。

The global mean travel time in Definition~7 is an episode-level evaluation
objective and is available only after a simulator episode is completed.
Imagined policy optimization instead requires dense credit at every route
decision.  We construct this credit from per-vehicle travel-time costs.  These per-vehicle rewards provide the
primary actor-learning signal, whereas the global critic supplies an auxiliary
aggregate value signal.
% 中文注释：Definition~7 中的 global mean travel time 是 episode-level evaluation objective，只有 simulator episode 完成后才能获得。相比之下，imagined policy optimization 需要在每次 route decision 时获得 dense credit。我们根据 per-CAV travel-time costs 构造这种 credit，并按照 vehicle identity 串联 returns。per-vehicle returns 提供主要的 actor-learning signal，而 global critic 提供辅助的 aggregate value signal，simulator evaluation 仍然是 checkpoint selection 的判据。

\subsubsection{Vehicle-Level Imagination}

The flow-matching dynamics model predicts traffic representations.
To initialize an imagined rollout, we first sample a replay episode \(e\)
uniformly and then randomly select an eligible decision epoch
\(t_{\mathrm{start}}\).  Let \(L_{\mathrm{im}}\) denote the imagination
horizon.  The aligned traffic field is encoded as
\(Z_{t_{\mathrm{start}}}^{(e)}=
f_\phi(X_{t_{\mathrm{start}}}^{(e)},G)\), while vehicle metadata from the same
epoch initializes the current segment, destination, selected route suffix,
elapsed time, and arrival status of each vehicle.  Starting from this sampled
state, the actor and world model generate the imagined trajectory for
\(t=t_{\mathrm{start}},\ldots,t_{\mathrm{start}}+L_{\mathrm{im}}-1\).
% 中文注释：flow-matching dynamics model 预测 aggregate traffic representations。为了初始化 imagined rollout，我们首先均匀采样一个 replay episode \(e\)，然后随机选择一个符合条件的 decision epoch \(t_{\mathrm{start}}\)。令 \(L_{\mathrm{im}}\) 表示 imagination horizon。对齐的 traffic field 被编码为 \(Z_{t_{\mathrm{start}}}^{(e)}=f_\phi(X_{t_{\mathrm{start}}}^{(e)},G)\)，同一时刻的 vehicle metadata 则用于初始化每辆车的 current segment、destination、selected route suffix、elapsed time 和 arrival status。从这个采样状态开始，actor 和 world model 生成 \(t=t_{\mathrm{start}},\ldots,t_{\mathrm{start}}+L_{\mathrm{im}}-1\) 上的 imagined trajectory。

At each imagined decision epoch, the actor selects a route and the decoder
recovers the predicted speed \(\widehat v_{t,u}\) of each road segment from
\(Z_t\).  The predicted travel time of road segment \(u\) is
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\widehat\tau_t(u)
&=&
\displaystyle
\frac{\ell(u)}{\widehat v_{t,u}}.
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Here \(\ell(u)\) is the segment length, so \(\widehat\tau_t(u)\) estimates the
time required to traverse segment \(u\) under the imagined traffic state.  During a fixed time interval of \(\Delta t\),
each vehicle travels along the selected route by consuming these segment travel
times in route order, while the learned transition predicts \(Z_{t+1}\) and starts the next decision epoch.
Because the wrapper advances vehicles with the same decoded travel-time
readout that the decoder derives from \(Z_t\), vehicle positions and the
segment traffic field evolve from a shared prediction rather than from two
independent state estimates.
% 中文注释：在每个 imagined decision epoch，actor 选择一条 route，decoder 则从 \(Z_t\) 恢复每条 road segment 的 predicted speed \(\widehat v_{t,u}\)。road segment \(u\) 的 predicted travel time 为 \(\widehat\tau_t(u)=\ell(u)/\widehat v_{t,u}\)。这里，\(\ell(u)\) 是 segment length，因此 \(\widehat\tau_t(u)\) 估计车辆在 imagined traffic state 下通过 segment \(u\) 所需的时间。deterministic vehicle wrapper 为每辆 active CAV 提供 \(\Delta t\) 的时间预算，并按照 route 顺序消耗这些 segment travel times，使车辆沿 selected route 前进；与此同时，learned transition 预测 \(Z_{t+1}\)。随后，wrapper 为下一次决策重新生成 current-to-destination candidate routes。

At the beginning of the next imagined decision epoch, the vehicles reaching departure time are added, and the arrived vehicles are removed. The remaining
CAVs form \(\mathcal{I}_{t+1}^{\mathrm{im}}\) and contribute actor
samples, rewards, and per-vehicle bootstrap values.
% 中文注释：在下一个 imagined decision epoch 开始时，wrapper 加入 scheduled departure time 已经到达的 CAV，并移除已经到达或不存在有效 current-to-destination candidate route 的 CAV。其余车辆构成 \(\mathcal{I}_{t+1}^{\mathrm{im}}\)，并且只有这些车辆会产生 actor samples、rewards 和 per-vehicle bootstrap values。该 wrapper 在不要求 graph-level world model 预测 per-vehicle terminal variables 的情况下显式保留 vehicle identity。

\subsubsection{Parameter-Shared CAV Route Actor}

The segment latent \(z_{t,u}\) contains the traffic attributes used to evaluate
candidate routes.  Because a candidate can contain a variable number of road
segments, UrbanDreamer converts its segment latents into a fixed-dimensional
representation by mean pooling:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\varphi_{i,t,k}
&=&
\displaystyle
\frac{1}{|P^k_{i,t}|}
\sum_{u\in P^k_{i,t}}z_{t,u}.
\end{array}
\end{equation}
% 中文注释：segment latent \(z_{t,u}\) 包含用于评价 candidate routes 的 traffic attributes。由于一条 candidate route 可以包含数量可变的 road segments，UrbanDreamer 通过 mean pooling 将其 segment latents 转换为 fixed-dimensional representation：\(\varphi_{i,t,k}=|P^k_{i,t}|^{-1}\sum_{u\in P^k_{i,t}}z_{t,u}\)。

The context shared by all active CAVs summarizes the graph and decision
progress:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\bar z_t
&=&
\displaystyle
\frac{1}{|N|}\sum_{u\in N}z_{t,u},\\[0.35em]
g_t
&=&
\displaystyle
\left[
\bar z_t,
\frac{t}{T},
\frac{|\mathcal{I}_t|}{|N^{\mathrm{CAV}}|}
\right].
\end{array}
\end{equation}
The vectorized realization of the observation operator is therefore
\begin{equation}
o_{i,t}
=
\big[
\varphi_{i,t,1}\Vert\cdots\Vert
\varphi_{i,t,K}\Vert g_t
\big],
\end{equation}
where \(|N^{\mathrm{CAV}}|\) is the number of CAVs scheduled in the episode and
\(\Vert\) denotes concatenation.
% 中文注释：所有 active CAVs 共享的 context 概括 graph 和 decision progress：\(\bar z_t=|N|^{-1}\sum_{u\in N}z_{t,u}\)，\(g_t=[\bar z_t,t/T,|\mathcal{I}_t|/|N^{\mathrm{CAV}}|]\)。这里，\(|N^{\mathrm{CAV}}|\) 是 episode 中 scheduled CAVs 的数量。因此，observation operator 的 vectorized realization 为 \(o_{i,t}=[\varphi_{i,t,1}\Vert\cdots\Vert\varphi_{i,t,K}\Vert g_t]\)，其中 \(\Vert\) 表示 concatenation。

The learned policy must select one executable route at deployment.  UrbanDreamer
applies the parameter-shared actor to every CAV and every candidate, and
normalizes the route scores into a categorical distribution over the valid
candidates:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\pi_\theta(k\mid o_{i,t})
&=&
\mathrm{Softmax}_{k:m_{i,t,k}=1}
\!\left(f_\theta^{\mathrm{route}}(\varphi_{i,t,k},g_t)\right).
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Candidates with \(m_{i,t,k}=0\) are excluded from the softmax, and invalid
slots are masked out when fewer than \(K\) candidates exist.  Parameter sharing across
CAVs keeps the policy applicable to a variable number of active vehicles, and
sharing the actor across candidate slots makes the policy depend on route
features.
% 中文注释：learned policy 必须在部署时选择一条可执行的 route。因此，UrbanDreamer 对每辆 CAV 和每条 candidate 使用同一个 route scorer，并令 \(\pi_\theta(k\mid o_{i,t})=\mathrm{Softmax}_{k:m_{i,t,k}=1}(f_\theta^{\mathrm{route}}(\varphi_{i,t,k},g_t))\)。\(m_{i,t,k}=0\) 的 candidates 不参与 softmax；候选数不足 \(K\) 时无效 slot 被 mask。CAV 之间的 parameter sharing 使 policy 能适用于可变数量的 active vehicles，而 candidate slots 之间共享 scorer，使 score 取决于 route features 而不是 slot position。


For policy optimization, let \(\pi_{\mathrm{old}}\) denote the behavior policy
that produced \(a_{i,t}\).  The per-vehicle and global critics introduced below
provide the value estimates \(v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}\) and
\(v_t^{\mathrm{global}}\), together with their corresponding return targets
\(G_{i,t}^{\lambda}\) and \(G_t^{\mathrm{global}}\).  The detached advantages,
their actor combination, and its standardized clipped form are
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}
&=&
\mathrm{sg}\!\left(
G_{i,t}^{\lambda}-v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}
\right),\\
A_t^{\mathrm{global}}
&=&
\mathrm{sg}\!\left(
G_t^{\mathrm{global}}-v_t^{\mathrm{global}}
\right),\\
A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{actor}}
&=&
A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}+\alpha_g A_t^{\mathrm{global}},\\
\bar A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{actor}}
&=&
\displaystyle
\mathrm{clip}\!\left(
\frac{A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{actor}}-\mu_A}{\sigma_A+\varepsilon_A},-c,c
\right).
\end{array}
\end{equation}
The current-to-behavior probability ratio and its clipped counterpart are
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\rho_{i,t}
&=&
\displaystyle
\frac{\pi_\theta(a_{i,t}\mid o_{i,t})}
{\pi_{\mathrm{old}}(a_{i,t}\mid o_{i,t})},\\[0.35em]
\bar\rho_{i,t}
&=&
\mathrm{clip}(\rho_{i,t},1-\epsilon,1+\epsilon).
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Here \(\mu_A\) and \(\sigma_A\) are minibatch statistics,
\(\varepsilon_A>0\) stabilizes normalization, and \(c\) and \(\epsilon\) are
the advantage and probability-ratio clipping thresholds.  Moreover,
\(\mathrm{sg}\) stops gradients through the return targets, and \(\alpha_g\)
weights the global credit.  The global term is shared by CAV actions from the
same imagined epoch, so policy improvement internalizes network-wide congestion
while retaining vehicle-specific credit.
% 中文注释：在 policy optimization 中，令 \(\pi_{\mathrm{old}}\) 表示产生 \(a_{i,t}\) 的 behavior policy。下文的 per-vehicle critic 和 global critic 分别提供 value estimates \(v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}\)、\(v_t^{\mathrm{global}}\) 及其 return targets \(G_{i,t}^{\lambda}\)、\(G_t^{\mathrm{global}}\)。vehicle advantage 与 global advantage 分别为 \(A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}=\mathrm{sg}(G_{i,t}^{\lambda}-v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}})\) 和 \(A_t^{\mathrm{global}}=\mathrm{sg}(G_t^{\mathrm{global}}-v_t^{\mathrm{global}})\)。actor 使用 \(A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{actor}}=A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}+\alpha_gA_t^{\mathrm{global}}\)，并在组合后统一标准化和裁剪。这里，\(\mu_A\) 和 \(\sigma_A\) 是 minibatch statistics，\(\varepsilon_A\) 用于稳定 normalization，\(c\) 和 \(\epsilon\) 分别是 advantage 和 probability-ratio clipping thresholds，\(\mathrm{sg}\) 阻断 return targets 上的梯度，\(\alpha_g\) 控制 global credit 的权重。同一 imagined epoch 的 active CAVs 共享 global term，因此 policy update 同时保留 vehicle-specific credit 并纳入 network-wide congestion credit。

The actor minimizes the clipped discrete surrogate, where
\(\mathbb{E}_{i,t}\) averages over active CAV decisions in the imagined
minibatch:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\mathcal{L}_{\pi}
&=&
-\mathbb{E}_{i,t}
\left[
\min\!\left(
\rho_{i,t}\bar A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{actor}},
\bar\rho_{i,t}\bar A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{actor}}
\right)
\right]\\
&&
-\beta\mathbb{E}_{i,t}
\left[\mathcal{H}(\pi_\theta(\cdot\mid o_{i,t}))\right],
\end{array}
\end{equation}
and follows the policy-improvement direction
\begin{equation}
g_\theta^{\mathrm{actor}}
=
-\nabla_\theta\mathcal{L}_{\pi}.
\end{equation}
Here \(\mathcal H\) is the entropy of the categorical route distribution and
\(\beta\) is its regularization weight.  Thus the global critic affects the
actor through \(A_t^{\mathrm{global}}\), rather than only through a critic loss.
% 中文注释：actor 最小化使用组合 advantage \(\bar A_{i,t}^{\mathrm{actor}}\) 的 clipped discrete surrogate，其中 \(\mathbb{E}_{i,t}\) 对 imagined minibatch 中的 active CAV decisions 求平均；随后，actor 沿 policy-improvement direction \(g_\theta^{\mathrm{actor}}=-\nabla_\theta\mathcal{L}_{\pi}\) 更新。这里，\(\mathcal H\) 是 categorical route distribution 的 entropy，\(\beta\) 是它的 regularization weight。global critic 不再只产生 critic loss；它的 detached lambda advantage 直接进入 actor objective。


\subsubsection{Per-Vehicle Critic}

The per-vehicle critic first encodes every observation independently with a
parameter-shared multilayer perceptron (MLP) \(f_\eta^{\mathrm{enc}}\), and then
applies multi-head self-attention within the active CAV set:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
e_{i,t} &=& f_\eta^{\mathrm{enc}}(o_{i,t}),\\
(\widetilde e_{i,t})_{i\in\mathcal{I}_t}
&=&
\mathrm{Attention}_\eta((e_{i,t})_{i\in\mathcal{I}_t}),\\
v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}
&=&
w_v^\top\widetilde e_{i,t}+b_v.
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Here \(\mathrm{Attention}_\eta\) denotes multi-head self-attention. And \(v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}\) estimates the
future return of vehicle \(i\). 
% 中文注释：per-vehicle critic 首先使用 parameter-shared multilayer perceptron (MLP) \(f_\eta^{\mathrm{enc}}\) 独立编码每个 observation，然后在 active CAV set 内应用 multi-head self-attention：\(e_{i,t}=f_\eta^{\mathrm{enc}}(o_{i,t})\)，\((\widetilde e_{i,t})_{i\in\mathcal{I}_t}=\mathrm{Attention}_\eta((e_{i,t})_{i\in\mathcal{I}_t})\)，并输出 \(v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}=w_v^\top\widetilde e_{i,t}+b_v\)。attention layer 使每个 value estimate 能够考虑 simultaneous CAV decisions、shared bottlenecks 和 route overlap，同时仍为每辆车保留一个 scalar output。因此，\(v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}\) 估计车辆 \(i\) 的 future return，并作为 actor credit assignment 的主要 baseline。

For the route selected by CAV \(i\), let \(P_{i,t}^{\mathrm{rem}}\) denote its
suffix road segments.  The predicted remaining travel time and per-vehicle reward
are
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\widehat T_{i,t}^{\mathrm{rem}}
&=&
\displaystyle
\sum_{u\in P_{i,t}^{\mathrm{rem}}}
\widehat\tau_{t+1}(u),\\[0.35em]
\delta_{i,t}
&=&
\min(\Delta,\widehat T_{i,t}^{\mathrm{rem}}),\\
r_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}
&=&
-\delta_{i,t}/\kappa_r.
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Here \(\delta_{i,t}\) is the imagined travel time accumulated before the next
decision or arrival, and \(\kappa_r\) controls the reward scale.
% 中文注释：对于 CAV \(i\) 选择的 route，令 \(P_{i,t}^{\mathrm{rem}}\) 表示其尚未经过的 suffix。predicted remaining travel time 为 \(\widehat T_{i,t}^{\mathrm{rem}}=\sum_{u\in P_{i,t}^{\mathrm{rem}}}\widehat\tau_{t+1}(u)\)，per-vehicle reward 由 \(\delta_{i,t}=\min(\Delta t,\widehat T_{i,t}^{\mathrm{rem}})\) 和 \(r_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}=-\delta_{i,t}/\kappa_r\) 给出。这里，\(\delta_{i,t}\) 是在下一次决策或到达之前累计的 imagined travel time，\(\kappa_r\) 控制 reward scale。在 CAV 的 imagined lifetime 内累积该 reward，可得到其 predicted travel time 的 dense surrogate。

Returns are linked by vehicle identity.  Let
\(\zeta_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{act}}\in\{0,1\}\) indicate whether CAV \(i\) remains
in \(\mathcal{I}_{t+1}^{\mathrm{im}}\).
The masked bootstrap value, per-vehicle lambda return, and critic loss are
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
\widetilde v_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{veh}}
&=&
\zeta_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{act}}v_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{veh}},\\
G_{i,t}^{\lambda}
&=&
r_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}
+\gamma\left[
(1-\lambda)\widetilde v_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{veh}}
+\lambda G_{i,t+1}^{\lambda}
\right],\\[0.35em]
\mathcal{L}_{V}^{\mathrm{veh}}
&=&
\displaystyle
\frac{1}{2}\mathbb{E}_{i,t}
\left[
(v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}
-\mathrm{sg}(G_{i,t}^{\lambda}))^2
\right].
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Here \(\gamma\) is the discount factor and \(\lambda\) is the trace parameter.
Only observations of the same CAV are connected by this recursion.  Once the
vehicle leaves the active set, \(\zeta_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{act}}=0\) prevents
bootstrapping beyond its imagined lifetime.
% 中文注释：returns 按照 vehicle identity 连接。令 \(\zeta_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{act}}\in\{0,1\}\) 表示 CAV \(i\) 是否仍属于 \(\mathcal{I}_{t+1}^{\mathrm{im}}\)。masked bootstrap value、per-vehicle lambda return 和 critic loss 分别为 \(\widetilde v_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{veh}}=\zeta_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{act}}v_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{veh}}\)、\(G_{i,t}^{\lambda}=r_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}+\gamma[(1-\lambda)\widetilde v_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{veh}}+\lambda G_{i,t+1}^{\lambda}]\)，以及 \(\mathcal{L}_{V}^{\mathrm{veh}}=\frac{1}{2}\mathbb{E}_{i,t}[(v_{i,t}^{\mathrm{veh}}-\mathrm{sg}(G_{i,t}^{\lambda}))^2]\)。这里，\(\gamma\) 是 discount factor，\(\lambda\) 是 trace parameter。该递推只连接同一辆 CAV 的 observations。车辆离开 active set 后，\(\zeta_{i,t+1}^{\mathrm{act}}=0\)，因此不会在其 imagined lifetime 之外继续 bootstrap。

\subsubsection{Global Critic}

The auxiliary global critic reuses the graph representation \(\bar z_t\)
together with a decision context \(c_t\) containing the normalized decision
time and the active CAV and HDV fractions:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
v_t^{\mathrm{global}}
&=&
V_\omega^{\mathrm{global}}
(\bar z_t,c_t).
\end{array}
\end{equation}
The planned CAV pressure \(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}\) conditions only the
world-model transition and never enters a value baseline, so
\(v_t^{\mathrm{global}}\) is an action-independent estimate of the
network-level state value.  The global critic captures aggregate congestion
effects that cannot be assigned to one CAV observation alone.  We retain this
estimate for auxiliary network-level value learning, but the actor's primary
advantage is computed from the per-vehicle critic.
% 中文注释：辅助 global critic 复用 graph representation \(\bar z_t\)，并以 decision context \(c_t\)（归一化决策时间、active CAV/HDV 比例）为条件：\(v_t^{\mathrm{global}}=V_\omega^{\mathrm{global}}(\bar z_t,c_t)\)。planned CAV pressure \(\Psi_t^{\mathrm{CAV}}\) 只作为 world-model transition 的条件，不进入任何 value baseline，因此 \(v_t^{\mathrm{global}}\) 是 action-independent 的 network-level state value 估计，PPO advantage 的 baseline 无偏性成立。global critic 用于捕捉无法仅归因于某一辆 CAV observation 的 aggregate congestion effects；actor 的主要 advantage 由 per-vehicle critic 计算。


The world model carries a supervised head \(\widehat T_{\mathrm{all}}(z)\)
that predicts the completed-vehicle episode mean travel time of Definition~7
from a latent traffic state.  The global reward is the potential difference of
this readout along an imagined transition:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
r_t^{\mathrm{global}}
&=&
\kappa_g\left(
\widehat T_{\mathrm{all}}(z_t)
-
\gamma\,\widehat T_{\mathrm{all}}(z_{t+1})
\right).
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Here \(\kappa_g\) controls the reward scale.  With the potential
\(\Phi(z)=-\widehat T_{\mathrm{all}}(z)\), the discounted sum of
\(r_t^{\mathrm{global}}\) telescopes to the terminal predicted episode travel
time, so the global signal directly tracks the episode objective instead of an
instantaneous congestion proxy.  It complements, rather than replaces,
simulator evaluation.
% 中文注释：world model 带有一个 supervised head \(\widehat T_{\mathrm{all}}(z)\)，从 latent traffic state 预测 Definition~7 的 completed-vehicle episode mean travel time。global reward 是该 readout 沿 imagined transition 的 potential difference：\(r_t^{\mathrm{global}}=\kappa_g(\widehat T_{\mathrm{all}}(z_t)-\gamma\widehat T_{\mathrm{all}}(z_{t+1}))\)。取 potential \(\Phi(z)=-\widehat T_{\mathrm{all}}(z)\) 时，\(r_t^{\mathrm{global}}\) 的折扣和 telescopes 到终止状态的预测 episode travel time，因此 global signal 直接追踪 episode 目标，而不是瞬态 congestion proxy；它补充而不是替代 simulator evaluation。

The global critic is trained from the corresponding lambda return:
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{rcl}
G_t^{\mathrm{global}}
&=&
r_t^{\mathrm{global}}
+\gamma\left[
(1-\lambda)v_{t+1}^{\mathrm{global}}
+\lambda G_{t+1}^{\mathrm{global}}
\right],\\[0.35em]
\mathcal{L}_{V}^{\mathrm{global}}
&=&
\displaystyle
\frac{1}{2}\mathbb{E}_{t}
\left[
(v_t^{\mathrm{global}}
-\mathrm{sg}(G_t^{\mathrm{global}}))^2
\right].
\end{array}
\end{equation}
Here \(\mathbb{E}_t\) averages over imagined decision epochs.  This objective
trains the global critic to summarize network-wide rollout quality.  Its detached residual
\(A_t^{\mathrm{global}}=\mathrm{sg}(G_t^{\mathrm{global}}-v_t^{\mathrm{global}})\)
enters every active CAV's actor advantage at epoch \(t\), weighted by
\(\alpha_g\).
% 中文注释：global critic 使用对应的 lambda return 训练，其中 \(G_t^{\mathrm{global}}=r_t^{\mathrm{global}}+\gamma[(1-\lambda)v_{t+1}^{\mathrm{global}}+\lambda G_{t+1}^{\mathrm{global}}]\)，并且 \(\mathcal{L}_{V}^{\mathrm{global}}=\frac{1}{2}\mathbb{E}_{t}[(v_t^{\mathrm{global}}-\mathrm{sg}(G_t^{\mathrm{global}}))^2]\)。这里，\(\mathbb{E}_t\) 对 imagined decision epochs 求平均。该 objective 训练 global critic 概括 network-wide rollout quality；其 detached residual \(A_t^{\mathrm{global}}=\mathrm{sg}(G_t^{\mathrm{global}}-v_t^{\mathrm{global}})\) 以权重 \(\alpha_g\) 进入该 epoch 每辆 active CAV 的 actor advantage。
