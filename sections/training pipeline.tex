\section{Training Pipeline}
\label{app:training-pipeline}

UrbanDreamer trains in two stages: an offline warm-up that initializes the
world model, and an episode-wise online stage that alternates simulator
collection, world-model updates, and imagined actor--critic optimization.

\paragraph{World-model warm-up.}
Before policy learning, we collect a fixed set of exploratory episodes in
which every CAV follows an \(\epsilon\)-greedy routing rule over the candidate
set, and fit the encoder, flow-matching dynamics, and decoder on the collected
transitions by minimizing \(\mathcal{L}_\phi\).  The actor, critics, and
response predictor are initialized at the start of the online stage.
% 中文注释：策略学习之前，先收集一组固定的探索性 episodes（每辆 CAV 使用 epsilon-greedy 选路），并用 \(\mathcal{L}_\phi\) 拟合 encoder、flow-matching dynamics 和 decoder。actor、critics 和 response predictor 在 online stage 开始时初始化。

\paragraph{Episode-wise online learning.}
Each online episode \(e\) runs the following cycle.  The current actor samples
a masked route for every active CAV; the same sampled one-hot route is
executed in the simulator, aggregated into \(\Psi_{e,t}^{\mathrm{CAV}}\), and
stored in the bounded replay buffer \(\mathcal{B}\).  After the episode, HDV
route probabilities are updated once
(Appendix~\ref{app:hdv-adaptation}).  The causal
response predictor then computes \(\hat H_e^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) from summaries of
the preceding episodes and attaches it to the stored transitions, after which
\(g_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) is updated on the available adjacent episode pairs by
\(\mathcal{L}_\psi\), and the world model is updated on replay minibatches by
\(\mathcal{L}_\phi\).  Finally, imagined actor--critic updates sample replay
states, roll out \(L_{\mathrm{im}}\) imagined decisions with the stored HDV
context, and update the actor and both critics from the per-vehicle and global
lambda returns.  The actor is not updated on real simulator returns.
% 中文注释：每个 online episode 执行以下循环：current actor 为每辆 active CAV 采样 masked route，同一个 one-hot route 用于执行、\(\Psi_{e,t}^{\mathrm{CAV}}\) 和 replay；episode 结束后按 Gawron 更新一次 HDV 路线概率；response predictor 仅用先前 episodes 的 summaries 计算 \(\hat H_e^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) 并附加到存储的 transitions 上，随后分别用 \(\mathcal{L}_\psi\) 和 \(\mathcal{L}_\phi\) 更新 response predictor 和 world model；最后从 replay 初始化 imagined rollouts，用 lambda returns 更新 actor 和两个 critics。actor 不直接使用真实仿真回报更新。

\paragraph{Policy selection.}
At designated episodes, UrbanDreamer saves the actor, critics, world model,
and response predictor together.  After training, each saved actor is
evaluated in the simulator with the same masked stochastic route sampling used
in collection, and the checkpoint with the lowest global mean travel time is
returned.
% 中文注释：在指定 episodes 保存全部模型。训练结束后，每个 saved actor 用与采集相同的 masked stochastic 采样在 simulator 中评估，返回 global mean travel time 最低的 checkpoint。

\begin{algorithm}[t]
\caption{Online training of UrbanDreamer}
\label{alg:urbandreamer}
\small
\begin{algorithmic}
\STATE \textbf{Warm-up:} collect exploratory episodes; fit world model by \(\mathcal{L}_\phi\)
\FOR{each online episode \(e\)}
  \STATE Collect episode with \(\pi_\theta\); store transitions in \(\mathcal{B}\)
  \STATE Update HDV route probabilities (Gawron)
  \STATE Predict \(\hat H_e^{\mathrm{HDV}}\); update \(g_\psi^{\mathrm{HDV}}\) by \(\mathcal{L}_\psi\)
  \STATE Update world model on replay minibatches by \(\mathcal{L}_\phi\)
  \FOR{each imagined update}
    \STATE Roll out \(L_{\mathrm{im}}\) imagined steps from replay states
    \STATE Update actor and critics by lambda returns
  \ENDFOR
\ENDFOR
\STATE Evaluate saved actors in the simulator; return the best
\end{algorithmic}
\end{algorithm}
